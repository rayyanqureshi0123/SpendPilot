## FAQ

**Q1: Is this really free?**
Yes. SpendPilot is 100% free to use. We're funded by Credex, which sells discounted AI software credits. If we find big savings in your stack, we'll offer to help — but there's zero obligation.

**Q2: How accurate is the audit?**
Every price in our engine is sourced from official vendor pricing pages (cited in our repo). The math is deterministic — not AI-generated. We only use AI to write the summary paragraph, never to calculate your savings.

**Q3: What tools do you support?**
Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude (Anthropic), ChatGPT (OpenAI), Anthropic API, OpenAI API, Google Gemini, and Windsurf. We update pricing weekly.

**Q4: Do you store my data?**
Your form inputs are stored locally in your browser until you choose to save your report. When you enter your email, we store the audit result (not your form inputs) in an encrypted database. The shareable public URL strips all identifying information.

**Q5: What is Credex?**
Credex sells discounted credits for enterprise AI tools — Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT Enterprise, and others — sourced from companies that overforecast or pivoted. The discount is real, typically 20–30% off retail.

# Unit Economics

## What Is a Converted Lead Worth to Credex?

Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits. A typical enterprise customer:
- Spends **$5,000–$15,000/month** on AI tools (Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT Enterprise across a 20–50 person team)
- Credex offers a **20–30% discount** on retail pricing via surplus credits
- Credex's margin on the discount spread: estimated **10–15%** (they buy at 40% off, sell at 20–30% off)
- **Average contract value:** $8,000/month × 12 months × 12% margin = **$11,520 gross profit per customer per year**
- **Customer lifetime:** 2 years average (switching costs are high once credits are integrated) = **$23,040 LTV**

Even at the low end (small startup, $2,000/mo spend, 10% margin, 1-year retention): **LTV = $2,400.**

## Conversion Funnel Math

```
1,000 visitors
  └── 400 audit completions (40% completion rate)
        └── 100 email captures (25% capture rate)
              └── 50 qualified leads with >$100/mo savings (50%)
                    └── 10 Credex consultations booked (20%)
                          └── 3 credit purchases closed (30% close rate)
```

**Revenue per 1,000 visitors:** 3 customers × $8,000/mo × 12% margin × 12 months = **$34,560**

## Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months

$1M ARR at $11,520 gross profit per customer = **87 paying customers needed.**

At our funnel rates (3 customers per 1,000 visitors), we need **29,000 total visitors** over 18 months, or roughly **1,600 visitors/month.**

**What would have to be true:**
1. **HN + Reddit + IH drive 800 organic visitors/month** (achievable with bi-monthly Show HN posts and weekly Reddit content)
2. **Credex email list grows to 5,000** and drives 400 visitors/month via quarterly re-engagement campaigns
3. **SEO on "AI tool pricing comparison" keywords** matures by month 6 and drives 400 visitors/month
4. **Close rate holds at 30%** — requires Credex sales team to follow up within 24 hours of a high-savings audit
5. **Average deal size stays above $5,000/mo** — below this, the funnel needs 2x more volume

The biggest risk is close rate. If it drops to 10% (realistic for a cold self-serve lead), we need 3x the traffic — roughly 5,000 visitors/month. That requires paid channels (LinkedIn ads targeting CTOs, ~$50 CPC, $2,500/month budget) which changes the CAC math significantly but is still profitable at $2,400+ LTV.
